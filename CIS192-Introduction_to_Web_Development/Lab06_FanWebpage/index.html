My Chemical Romance Fan Webpage

My Chemical Rommance is an alternative rockband from New Jersey. The band formed in 2001 and recently returned from a six year hiatus in 2019. The band is made up of brothers Gerard and Mikey Way along with guitarists Ray Toro and Frank Iero.

You can learn more about the band from their wikipedia article and find out about their latest shows at the official My Chemical Romance website.
